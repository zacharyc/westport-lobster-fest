Westport Rotary

Lobster Fest

Come join us for the first ever, annual, Westport Rotary Lobster Fest on September 22nd at Compo Beach between 3:00pm - 7:00pm. There will be lobster, steak, and all the beer and wine you want. There are children options as well. Only 750 tickets are available, so get yours today.

Menu

Lobster - $40

Two 1.25 pound lobsters plus corn, red potatoes, cole slaw, bread and butter, peppermint patties, and all the beer or wine you want, featuring Beaver Beer.

Steak - $40

A 14 oz. NY Strip Steak plus corn, red potatoes, cole slaw, bread and butter, peppermint patties, and all the beer or wine you want, featuring Beaver Beer.

Child's Meal - $10

Children ages 3-12 can choose from Hot Dogs, Chicken Fingers, Chips, and water or soda
